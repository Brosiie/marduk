      # Lapis Bay

      **Region ID:** `lapis_bay`
      **Recommended level range:** 12–22
      **Boss:** `raid_captain`
      **Palette:** Slate-blue · pirate-flag black · pale lapis

      ## Atmosphere

      Storm-grey port chained with rusted hawsers. Drowned sailors crawl up at low tide. Black-sail crews drink in shacks. Salt-witches pay better than the Crown.

      ## Mobs that spawn here

        - `raider_grunt`
- `raider_archer`
- `blood_hunter`
- `binding_construct`
