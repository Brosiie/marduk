      # Mist Vale

      **Region ID:** `mist_vale`
      **Recommended level range:** 35–50
      **Boss:** `tower_warden`
      **Palette:** Pearl-grey · ghost-white · gauze-pale

      ## Atmosphere

      Mist Breathing monks went too far in. The Walker in the Fog at Dusk wears your neighbor's face. Pilgrims who came to be forgiven sometimes stay. Visibility 8 meters.

      ## Mobs that spawn here

        - `binding_construct`
- `shrine_acolyte`
- `blood_hunter`
