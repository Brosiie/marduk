      # Bone Mountains

      **Region ID:** `bone_mountains`
      **Recommended level range:** 18–30
      **Boss:** `tower_warden`
      **Palette:** Bone-white · scree-grey · ice-blue

      ## Atmosphere

      Charnel-road peaks. The mountains are stitched from the bones of the people who built them. Stone Priests refuse to die. The first surveyor still climbs.

      ## Mobs that spawn here

        - `binding_construct`
- `shrine_acolyte`
- `raider_grunt`
