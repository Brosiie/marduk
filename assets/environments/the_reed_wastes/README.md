      # The Reed Wastes

      **Region ID:** `the_reed_wastes`
      **Recommended level range:** 5–15
      **Boss:** `raid_captain`
      **Palette:** Dust-yellow · salt-white · burnt sienna

      ## Atmosphere

      Marsh that forgot it was marsh. Heat shimmer rises off cracked salt flats; reeds rustle dryly with no breeze. Pilgrims who got lost in the cracking still walk circles. Hot light, no shadow.

      ## Mobs that spawn here

        - `raider_grunt`
- `raider_archer`
- `binding_construct`
- `shrine_acolyte`
